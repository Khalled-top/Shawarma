##Shawarma Security Threat Model:
##
Threat 1:
A hacker tries to steal the secret sauce recipe.

Impact:
Loss of confidentiality because the secret recipe becomes public.

Mitigation:
Encrypt the recipe and store it securely.

##
Threat 2:
Someone orders shawarma without garlic sauce !!!!

Security Impact:
Integrity issue because the original shawarma configuration was modified.

Mitigation:
Verify customer preferences before preparing the order.

##
Threat 3:
The shawarma machine rans out of power during lunch time.

Security Impact:
Availability issue because the service becomes unavailable.

Mitigation:
Use backup power systems.

##
Threat 4:
A fake shawarma shop creates a copy of the original recipe.

Security Impact:
Brand impersonation and reputation damage.

Mitigation:
Use authentication methods and protect the original recipe.

##
Threat 5:
An employee accidentally shares the secret sauce password.

Security Impact:
Unauthorized access to sensitive information.

Mitigation:
Apply access control and limit who can view secrets.

##
Threat 6:
A customer tries to receive extra shawarma without a valid order.

Security Impact:
Unauthorized access attempt.

Mitigation:
Implement order verification before serving.

##
Threat 7:
The shawarma delivery system gets attacked by too many fake orders.

Security Impact:
Denial of Service (DoS) because real customers cannot place orders.

Mitigation:
Use rate limiting and traffic monitoring.
